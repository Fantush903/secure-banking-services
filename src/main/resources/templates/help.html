Frequently Asked Questions

🔒 How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email, verify the OTP, and set a new password.

💸 What is the daily transfer limit?

The daily transfer limit is ₹1,00,000 via NEFT/IMPS. RTGS is available for amounts above ₹2,00,000.

📱 What is 2FA authentication?

Two-factor authentication sends a 6-digit OTP to your registered email on every login for extra security.

🏠 How long does loan approval take?

Loan applications are reviewed within 2-3 business days. You will be notified via email once approved.

🔓 My account is locked. What do I do?

Accounts are locked after 3 failed login attempts. Use "Forgot Password" to reset or contact admin.

📋 How do I download my statement?

Go to Transactions → click "Download Statement" to export your transaction history as PDF.
